Acme Robotics is a fictional company founded in 2019 in Girona, Catalonia. It
designs and manufactures small autonomous warehouse robots. Its headquarters
are in Girona, with a second assembly plant in Lleida.

The flagship product is the Pallet Pup, a knee-high mobile robot that ferries
pallets between shelving and loading docks. The Pallet Pup has a top speed of
2.4 metres per second and a battery that lasts nine hours on a single charge.

Acme Robotics employs 140 people. Its CEO is Berta Comas, a former logistics
engineer. The company's motto is "small robots, heavy lifting."

In 2024 Acme shipped 3,200 Pallet Pup units, mostly to fruit and textile
warehouses across southern Europe. The robots run on an in-house operating
system called PupOS.
